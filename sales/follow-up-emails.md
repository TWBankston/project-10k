## Tips for Using These Templates

### Timing
- **After call (no decision):** Same day or next morning
- **After proposal:** Day 2-3, then Day 5-7
- **Gone quiet:** Day 10-14
- **Win-back:** 30-60 days later

### Personalization Checklist
- [ ] Replace [BRACKETED] items
- [ ] Reference something specific from your conversation
- [ ] Include their business name
- [ ] Match their vibe (casual vs. professional)

### Subject Line Best Practices
- Short (under 50 characters)
- Create curiosity or urgency
- Avoid spammy words (FREE, URGENT, LIMITED TIME)
- Personal when possible

### What NOT to Do
- Don't send more than 4-5 follow-ups
- Don't be passive-aggressive
- Don't apologize for following up
- Don't write novels — keep it scannable
