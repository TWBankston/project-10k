# Cold Email Sequence - Home Services WaaS

**Target:** Home service business owners (landscaping, cleaning, pest control, etc.)
**Goal:** Book a 15-minute discovery call
**Sending volume:** 100-150/day per warmed domain

---

**Subject Lines (A/B test):**
- Quick question about [Company Name]
- [First Name], saw your business online
- Your website vs. your competitors

If you're curious what that could look like for [Company Name], I'd love to show you. 15 minutes, no pitch.

## Technical Setup Notes

### Domain Strategy
- Buy 3 domains similar to tbdesigned.io (e.g., tbdesigned.co, tbdesign.io, etc.)
- Warm each domain for 2 weeks before sending
- Max 50 emails/day per domain to start, scale to 100

### Warmup Process
1. Week 1: Send 10-20 emails/day to test addresses
2. Week 2: Increase to 30-40/day, mix of test + real recipients
3. Week 3: Full volume (50-100/day per domain)

### Tools
- **Instantly.ai** — Cold email sending + warmup ($37/mo)
- **Hunter.io or NeverBounce** — Email verification
- **Calendly** — Free tier works to start

### Tracking Metrics
- Open rate target: 40-60%
- Reply rate target: 3-8%
- Meeting booking rate: 0.5-1% of sends
